## Step by Step Guide to Sign Up and Download Microsoft Office 365

<h4> 1. Go to https://bit.ly/32s12g0 and enter your INTI student email address (xxx@student.newinti.edu.my). </h4>
<br>
<img src="./img/install-guides/guide-step-1.png" width="700">
<br><br>
<h4> 2. Click “I’m a student”. </h4>
<br>   
<img src="./img/install-guides/guide-step-2.png" width="700">
<br><br>
<h4> 3. Create your account and enter the verification code. Verification code has been sent to your INTI student email. Click “Start” to continue. </h4> 
<br>
<img src="./img/install-guides/guide-step-3.png" width="700">
<br><br>
<h4> 4. Click “Skip”. </h4>
<br>   
<img src="./img/install-guides/guide-step-4.png" width="700">
<br><br>
<h4> 5. Click “Install Office” and “Office 365 apps”. </h4>
<br>
<img src="./img/install-guides/guide-step-5.png" width="700">
<br><br>
<h4> 6. Wait for the installer to be downloaded. </h4>
<br>
<img src="./img/install-guides/guide-step-6.png" width="700">
<br><br>
<h4> 7. Run the downloaded installer and follow the instructions. </h4>
<br>
<img src="./img/install-guides/guide-step-7.png" width="700">
<br>
<h3> End </h3>

- To access your Microsoft Office 365 account in the future, go to http://portal.office.com and
sign in with your student account. 

- To install your Microsoft Office 365, follow the Step 5 – 7 to download and install Microsoft Office.

Reference link: https://sites.google.com/newinti.edu.my/intihub/get-office-365?authuser=0
